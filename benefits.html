benefits

The way we read is changing. We're on phones, tablets and desktops. We expect media-rich, interactive experiences. We demand data to understand our readers. That's how we designed Publet.

Publet is the way institutions communicate on any device, whether it's with 50 staff or five million customers. Our team comes from the media world—from Conde Nast to CNN—so we know what you need, from design to publishing to data. Now it's all in one place. Use Publet to make a brand guide, or as your company's platform for creating newsletters, proposals, white papers, annual reports, magazines, and more.

Anyone can create publications

Easy-to-use creation platform means non-technical staff can design and create.

  • Intuitive authoring tool
  • No training required. Start using in minutes
  • < 10% of cost and time compared to custom development
  • Keep branding and styles consistent across every publication (one-click update)

Reach phones, tablets and mobile seamlessly

HTML5 output makes your content look great on any device.

  • Deliver content through the browser for phones, tablets, and desktops. Integrate into your existing site or as a standalone publication with a unique URL
  • Fit any screen size, from wall to phone, with always-responsive content
  • Reach readers offline with options to send to e-reader or download as PDF
  • Share and publish content on the open web

Know your audience with built-in analytics

Publet's analytics let you track reader behavior to see how your content is being used and optimize it.

  • Line-by-line analytics reveal what content catches and what falls short
  • Test multiple versions of the same publication to boost lead generation and purchases
  • Understand audience desires (both staff and customers) by seeing what topics are useful, confusing or shared
  • Personalize content for individual users based on profile and behavior (coming soon)

FAQ

I don't see the specific publication type I need listed. Is Publet still right for me?

Publet can accomodate most publications that were once in print or PDF. Please contact us if you have specific questions about your publication needs.

What if I need readers to access my publications offline?

Users can save Publet publications as PDFs, or they can send them to Kindle, Nook or other e-readers for offline reading.

Where are Publet publications hosted?

You can host Publet publications on your own website, or we can host them for you.

Can I have my publications forever?

Yes, you can! In order to edit your publications, you need a Publet subscription, but you can download the code or PDF at any time and keep it forever.
