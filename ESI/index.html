Publet Summary

Publet lets ESI communicate effectively with its customers and employees on any digital device. With Publet, non-technical staff easily create and deliver digital content such as white papers, case studies and other publications using text, video, images, and audio. Each publication is automatically optimized for phones, tablets and desktops. ESI's marketing and communications staff recieve data to learn how to improve and customize that content for their audience.

  • No more messy emailed files.
  • No more ugly webpages.
  • No missed opportunities, so ESI can deliver its message & vision.
