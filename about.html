our team

michael coren

CEO

Michael is a science journalist and co-founder of Publet. He has reported for FastCompany, The Economist, Foreign Policy and others on science, economics, and the environment. Publet is his next adventure after serving as managing editor of Cambodia's Phnom Penh Post.

honza pokorny

lead developer

Honza develops software in his dreams, as well as his waking hours. He has written a lisp to js compiler and a redis clone in haskell. His favorite things are django, open source projects, and Czech beers. Pokorny serves as Publet's lead engineer.

rebecca power

developer

Power is a publishing specialist, software developer and storyteller. She is deeply familiar with every aspect of digital production, from source file to storefront, and has created multimedia publications hosted by some of the most innovative names in publishing. She once stole a beer from Harrison Ford in a bar.

dave stanton

Stanton has spent thousands of hours observing people reading the news. Stanton holds a PhD in news-reading behavior from University of Florida. He also teaches journalists how to code.
