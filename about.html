michael coren

CEO

Coren is a science journalist and co-founder of Publet. He reports for FastCompany, The Economist, Foreign Policy and others on science, economics, and the environment. Before serving as CNN.com's science producer, he was managing editor of Cambodia's Phnom Penh Post and earned an MESc focused on environmental economics from the Yale School of Forestry and Environmental Studies.

honza pokorny

lead developer

Excellent at developing software, Pokorny's favorite things include the django framework, open source projects, and Czech beers. Pokorny serves as our lead engineer. He is also an avid GeoCacher.

rebecca power

developer

Power is a publishing specialist, software developer and storyteller. She is deeply familiar with every aspect of digital production, from source file to storefront, and has created multimedia publications hosted by some of the most innovative names in publishing.

andy omel

designer

Omel has launched dozens of magazines, apps and redesigns including Conde Nast Traveler, Rolling Stone, Guitar World and Reader's Digest. He ensures every Publet publication is beautiful.

christie nicholson

community lead

Christie Nicholson has been helping to build online startups since 1999. She's also an award-winning science journalist and contributing editor at Scientific American, CBS's SmartPlanet and Core77. She is a board member of SXSW Interactive and the Science Media Center of Canada.
