how it works

How does Publet work?

Set your styles

Set your styles

  • Use one of our professionally designed style templates, create your own customized style template, or create a (free!) brand guide for your entire company to use. Add logos, assets, fonts, and colors. All files are ready for download.
  • Future publications are pre-populated with your styles.

Create content

  • Start creating minutes with Publet's intuitive authoring tool.
  • Add text, images, video, audio and even custom interactives.
  • Use our PDF parser to turn PDFs into responsive documents you edit immediately (Pro users).
  • Manage content across your organization to maintain consistency and empower your team.

Go everywhere

  • Spread your message with integrated social media sharing.

Know your audience

  • Access realtime data to track who reads your content, what they like, and how to improve it.
  • Apply A/B testing and optimization to multiply performance for lead generation, traffic and customer acquisition.
