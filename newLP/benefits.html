benefits

You're probably thinking to yourself, why Publet?

It is becoming more and more important for your employees, clients, and customers to be able to access their publications on mobile devices. Yet, it is expensive to create beautiful publications that are also responsive, meaning the content free-flows to various screen sizes. Publet solves this problem.

HTML5 output means that your content will reflow and look great on any device.

Built in analytics means you can track reader behavior to see how your content is being digested.

Easy to use creation platform means non-technical staff and design and create.

These three powerful aspects of Publet will allow you to increase sales while saving time and money.
